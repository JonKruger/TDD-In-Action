Feature: 		Deposit 
Description:	User should be able to deposit money into a bank account.

 
Feature:		Withdraw 
Description:	User should be able to withdraw money from a bank account along as there is enough money in the bank account for the withdrawal.

 
Feature:		Deposit Annual Interest
Description:	Multiply the account balance by the annual percentage rate and deposit that amount into the bank account.

 
Feature:		Close Account
Description:	The account balance must be 0 in order to close the account.  Once the account is closed, money cannot be deposited or withdrawn from the account.
